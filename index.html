Where I Started

This is the first app I ever made. I started learning about computer programming in the summer before my senior year (2014). I took the intro to Javascript course on KhanAcademy, and from there, I started programming one of my favorite card games into javascript so I could play it online. This Set Go app was the beginning point of my discovery of what programmers could do. Every little success made me so excited. I fell in love with what I could do. Now finally, after a couple of years of "real" programming in school, I brought the app out of KhanAcademy. Enjoy!
